Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn about how we handle your information.

Introduction

Gamekunda™ provides a variety of apps and services as free offerings. This policy outlines our practices concerning data collection, usage, and disclosure. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as per this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, we may request personal information, including Name, Phone, and Email. These details are retained on your device and are not collected by us.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services, like Google Play Services, which may collect data to identify you. We advise reviewing their privacy policies for further details.

3. Log Data

In case of errors, log data (such as IP address, device name, and OS version) may be collected through third-party tools to help improve our services.

4. Cookies

Our app may use cookies through third-party integrations. You have the option to accept or decline these cookies.

5. Service Providers

We may employ third-party providers for various services. They access your data solely for performing tasks and are required to maintain confidentiality.

6. Security

While we prioritize securing your personal information, please be aware that no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Links to Other Sites

Our services may link to external sites not operated by us. We strongly recommend reviewing their privacy policies, as we have no control over their practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you become aware of such data, please contact us to take necessary actions.

9. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Please check this page for any changes. This policy is effective as of January 1, 2021.
